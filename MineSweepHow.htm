Imagine a tic tac toe box. This is what you are looking at when you see the grid. You need to have tunnel vision! so look at the middle square of the tic tac toe grid. suppose its the common 1. This means that only 1 bomb is in the surrounding 8 squares.

 

 

You must then use information from other surrounding grids to complete the puzzle. When you're confident you've located a bomb, right click on it and it will be marked so with a red flag.

 

If you are forced to guess, always open a square with the least chance of hitting a mine.
